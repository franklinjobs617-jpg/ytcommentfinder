How to Find Your
YouTube Comment History

Last Updated: December 2025

Quick Summary:

  1. Go to your Google My Activity dashboard.
  2. Filter by 'YouTube History'.
  3. Select 'Comments' to view, manage, and delete.

1 Accessing via Desktop

Navigate to Google My Activity

The YouTube Comment History is managed through your main Google Account Activity dashboard. Click here to go directly to the official Google Support page.

Filter Interactions

Once there, look for the 'Filter by date and product' option. Select 'YouTube' as the product, then apply a second filter for 'Interactions' and specifically choose 'Comments'.

Expert Tip

"I've spent hundreds of hours in the YouTube data settings. Use the official Google Takeout tool for a full archive, but use the My Activity page for quick deletions—it's much faster."

2 Finding History on Mobile

App Settings

Tap your profile picture, then select 'Your data in YouTube' or 'Settings' > 'History & privacy'. This redirects you to the web dashboard.

Why Can't I Find Old Comments?

Deleted Content

If the video or channel was deleted, your comment is gone from the history log.

Spam Filters

Comments marked as spam by YouTube's AI often don't appear in your main history.

Note: If you are looking for specific comments made by other users, the official tool cannot help. You need a specialized YouTube Comment Search tool.

FAQ & Troubleshooting

Can I recover a deleted comment?

No, once deleted, it is permanently removed from YouTube's servers.

How long is history stored?

Until you delete it, or based on your auto-delete settings (3, 18, or 36 months).
