Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how YTCommentFinder.com ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our website and services (the "Service").

1. Data We Collect (Non-Personal Information)

We prioritize user privacy and do not collect Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as your name or email address, unless voluntarily provided (e.g., when contacting us).

Information Automatically Collected:

  • Usage Data: Details about how you use the Service, such as the URLs of YouTube videos/channels searched and the features used (e.g., AI Analysis, Data Export).
  • Technical Data: Your browser type, operating system, IP address (used for general geolocation), and device type.
  • Public YouTube Data: All data displayed on the Service (comments, user IDs, etc.) are derived exclusively from publicly available YouTube API sources.

2. Third-Party Advertising (Google AdSense Compliance)

We use third-party vendors, including Google, to serve ads when you visit our website. This is done to support the operation of our free Service.

  • Google AdSense: Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site.
  • DART Cookie: Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our Service and other sites on the Internet.
  • Opt-Out: Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network Privacy Policy: Google Ads Policy.
  • Cookies: These third-party advertisers may use cookies, JavaScript, or web beacons to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and personalize content. We have no access to or control over these cookies.

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our Service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect PII from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you learn that your child has provided us with PII, please contact us immediately at admin@Ytcommentfinder.com.

4. Links to Third-Party Privacy Policies

For compliance and transparency, we provide links to the privacy policies of essential partners:

  • Google (General/Analytics/AdSense): Google Privacy Policy
  • AI Service Providers: The privacy policy for our core AI analysis partner is available here: AI Partner Policy.

5. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us by email: admin@Ytcommentfinder.com
