The Ultimate Guide to Finding and Managing Your YouTube Comment History

Last Updated: December 2025

Quick Summary:

  1. Go to your Google My Activity dashboard.
  2. Filter by 'YouTube History'.
  3. Select 'Comments' to view, manage, and delete.

Step 1: Accessing Your Official YouTube History Page (Desktop)

YouTube's native settings can be tricky to navigate. Here is the most direct route to find your YouTube Comment History using a desktop browser:

1. Navigate to Google My Activity

The YouTube Comment History is managed through your main Google Account Activity dashboard. Click here to go directly to the official Google Support page.

2. Filter by YouTube and Interactions

Once there, look for the 'Filter by date and product' option. Select 'YouTube' as the product, then apply a second filter for 'Interactions' and specifically choose 'Comments'.

Filtering your Google My Activity page to show only comments

Expert Experience: Quick Deletion Tip

"I've spent hundreds of hours in the YouTube data settings. The best pro-tip I can give you is to use the official Google Takeout tool for a full archive, but use the My Activity page for quick deletions—it's much faster."

Step 2: Finding Your Comment History on Mobile (App)

The process on the mobile app is slightly different, but still directs you to the web history page. Here's the path for iOS and Android:

1. Go to Your Profile and Settings

Tap your profile picture, then select 'Your data in YouTube' or 'Settings' > 'History & privacy'.

2. View and Manage History

The mobile pathway to find your comment data

Why You Can't Find Old or Deleted Comments (Technical Limitations)

If you're missing comments, it's usually due to one of three reasons: 1) The comment was deleted by you or the creator; 2) The channel was deleted; 3) The comment was marked as spam.

If you are looking for specific comments made by other users, or comments that may have been deleted by the user but still exist in a third-party archive, the official tool cannot help. For finding other users' comments, you need a specialized YouTube Comment Search tool.

YouTube Comment History FAQ and Troubleshooting

Can I recover a deleted comment?

No, once you delete a comment from your history, it is permanently removed from YouTube's servers and cannot be recovered via the official tools.

How long does YouTube store my comment history?

By default, your comment history is stored until you manually delete it, though you can set up auto-deletion after 3, 18, or 36 months in your Google Activity controls.
