1

Google My Activity (Recommended)

Official Google Method

Google My Activity is the most reliable way to find your YouTube comments. It stores a complete history of all your interactions across Google services, including every comment you've posted.

1

Visit Google My Activity

Go to myactivity.google.com and sign in to your Google account.

2

Filter by YouTube

Click "Filter by date & product" → Select "YouTube" → Apply filter.

3

Search for Comments

Use the search box to find specific comments or browse chronologically.

Pro Tip

You can search for specific keywords within your comments using the search bar. Try searching for phrases you remember using, like "great video" or "thanks for sharing."

2

YouTube Studio (For Creators)

Creator Dashboard

If you're a content creator, YouTube Studio provides a comments section where you can see all comments on your videos, including your own replies.

  • View comments on your videos
  • Search and filter comments
  • Only shows your own channel's videos

Access via studio.youtube.com → Comments section

3

Browser History Search

Manual Detective Work

Your browser history can help you retrace videos where you left comments. This method works best if you remember approximately when you commented.

Chrome

Ctrl+H → Search "youtube.com"

Firefox

Ctrl+Shift+H → Filter by site

Safari

History menu → Search

4

Email Notifications Trail

Follow the Reply Chain

When someone replies to your comment, YouTube sends you an email notification. These emails contain links back to your original comment.

Search your email for:

Subject lines:

  • • "replied to your comment"
  • • "New reply on YouTube"
  • • "Someone replied to you"

From addresses:

  • • noreply@youtube.com
  • • notifications@youtube.com

Common Issues & Solutions

Can't Find Recent Comments

Your comment might be pending approval or caught in spam filters.

Solution: Check if the comment appears when you're logged in to the video.

Comments Disappeared

The video owner may have deleted your comment or the entire video was removed.

Solution: Check Google My Activity for a record of the deleted comment.

Old Comments Missing

Google My Activity might not show very old comments if you've changed privacy settings.

Solution: Try Google Takeout for a complete historical archive.

Privacy Concerns

Worried about your comment history being tracked?

Solution: Adjust your Google Activity settings or use incognito mode for future comments.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I see comments I left on private or deleted videos?

Google My Activity may still show records of comments on deleted videos, but you won't be able to access the actual video or comment thread.

How far back does Google My Activity go?

Google My Activity typically stores data indefinitely unless you manually delete it or have auto-delete settings enabled (3, 18, or 36 months).

Can I find comments from a different Google account?

No, Google My Activity only shows data for the currently signed-in account. You'll need to switch accounts to see comments from different profiles.

What if I can't remember which videos I commented on?

Use Google My Activity's search function with keywords you remember using in comments, or browse chronologically by date ranges when you were most active.
